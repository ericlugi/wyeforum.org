About World Young Economists' Forum

Where global economics meets the next generation.

Encourage Exchange, Enrich Economics.

A student-led forum connecting young thinkers with experts, executives and decision-makers in economics and finance.

The forum Focuses discussions on economics, finance, and global affairs to enrich young economists’ knowledge and expand their capabilities.

Each session is structured around a core question in economics, finance, or public policy. They are designed to encourage pensive thinking, profound discussion and critical reflection regarding current world affairs.

Speakers include researchers, executives, and practitioners from universities, policy institutions, and multinational organizations.
