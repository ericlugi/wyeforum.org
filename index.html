What the Forum focuses on
Focused discussions on economics, finance, and global affairs to enrich young economists’ knowledge and expand their capabilities.
Sessions

Shaped around questions

Each session is structured around a core question in economics, finance, or public policy. They are designed to encourage pensive thinking, profound discussion and critical reflection regarding current world affairs.

Speakers

Academics & industry leaders

Speakers include researchers, executives, and practitioners from universities, policy institutions, and multinational organizations.

Team

Student-led, independently organized

The forum is organized by a small student team responsible for speaker outreach, moderation, and program design.
