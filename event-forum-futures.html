Forum Futures 2025

Panel · 20 September 2025 · 18:00 – 19:30 · Hall B

Event overview

This panel looked at where forums and online communities are heading: from smaller, more intimate spaces to new tools for moderation and governance.

Together with practitioners, we discussed what we want to preserve from early internet culture – and what we should leave behind.
