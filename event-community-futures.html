Community Futures 2026

Keynote · 24 March 2026 · 19:00 – 20:30 · Main stage

Event overview

What does “community” look like in 5–10 years? In this keynote we'll explore scenarios for how online and offline spaces might evolve, and which practices we can start now to build healthier futures.

The session combines short input, a live poll and time for Q&A. No slides overload – just a grounded conversation with space for your questions.
